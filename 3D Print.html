About Us

Since the inception of Cube 3D Prints in 2018, our vision has been to establish ourselves as the premier destination for all SLA 3D printing requirements in Kochi and Kerala. Our goal is to foster a community of creators, designers, and innovators who are united by a passion for this technology and dedicated to expanding its potential.

We employ high-precision SLA/Resin 3D Printers to produce superior quality 3D Printed models for a range of applications in medical, dental, and engineering fields. We offer a wide range of 3D scan, 3D Design and 3D printing services, including prototyping, custom design, and production runs.
